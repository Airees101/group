The History of Cybersecurity

*The internet first started in 1969

*US Gov. recognized Cybersecurity in 2003

*By 2007 mobile computers (mobile phones) are carried by most people.

*First U.S. patent for cybersecurity came into effect on September 1983. MIT was granted this patent for cryptographic communications system and method, which then introduced the RSA (Rivest-Shamir-Adleman) algorith being one of the first public key cryptosystems!

*Under George W. Bush's administration in 2002 the Department of Homeland Security which was also responsible for the IT infrastructure. In return a separate division was then established specifically for cybersecurity.

*Hactivist group Anonymous then was created in 2003 that became known for their variety of cyber attacks agaisnt governments and other various organizations.

As for today the world of IT is expanding each second with endless amounts of possibilites. With the government spending $18 billion alone on cybersecurity to continue as our defense in stopping blackhat criminals. However, this does not mean that everyone is protected 100% as it is still expected for each person with internet access to be aware of what could be potentially compromised. As most attacks are done by phishing attempts through emails with malicious links leading resulting in downloads that can be virus, trojans, or even rootkits being installed on your computers. Due to this, it is very important to educate as much people to save them from becoming victims from these various attacks. With some of the simple things that can save you can include anti-virus software, not reusing passwords, updating vital softwares to protect you from exploits, and not clicking links from email accounts that look unordinary or asking for personal information.
